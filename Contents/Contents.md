# 🎯 Course Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this training programme, participants will be able to:

- ✅ Navigate Microsoft Excel efficiently.
- ✅ Create professional engineering worksheets.
- ✅ Apply formulas and built-in functions confidently.
- ✅ Organize and analyse engineering datasets.
- ✅ Develop charts and graphical representations.
- ✅ Prepare professional engineering reports.
- ✅ Solve real-world and basic Civil Engineering problems using Microsoft Excel.
